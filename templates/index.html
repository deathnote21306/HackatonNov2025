McGill Medical AI Assistant

Created during the MAIS Hacks 2025 Hackathon, this project explores how AI-driven agents can assist in medical contexts by answering patient calls, providing basic symptom guidance, and scheduling follow-up appointments.

Our goal is to build an intelligent system that helps healthcare professionals handle non-emergency calls efficiently, while ensuring accessibility and safety for all users.
